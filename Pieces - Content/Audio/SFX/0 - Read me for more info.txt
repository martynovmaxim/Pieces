Here are the detailed info about when to play each sound effect file:

BallHitsPiece: when the ball hits a mobile piece
BallHitsWall: when the ball hits a wall
BallShoot: when the ball is shot and starts moving towards a certain direction
PieceHitsWall: when a mobile piece hits a wall
ShootPowerRiser (1-8): when the power of the shot to the ball is being determined (after determining the direction and before shooting the ball)
